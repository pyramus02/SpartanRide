About Us

SpartanRide is a carpooling service made
with students of UNCG in mind.

Socializing is a big part of the College experience,
and it isn't always easy to make new friends.
Our service aims to allow students to save money,
meet new friends, and travel!

With SpartanRide, verified students are able to travel where
they please. All it takes is to sign up and log in as a student
and to set a destination. The choice to drive or ride along is
up to you!




Environment

SpartanRide is not only committed to
assisting students, but to the environment too.

Our hope with SpartanRide is to reduce
carbon emissions in Greensboro.
Increasing the number of riders with each
student's car trip will help largely lower
the amount of exhaust each car outputs into
the air.
